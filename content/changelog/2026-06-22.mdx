---
title: Schema libraries — Zod, Valibot & ArkType
description: Generate your validation schema with Zod, Valibot, or ArkType.
---

- **Schema library** — choose Zod, Valibot, or ArkType for the generated
  validation schema. It works with either form library (React Hook Form or
  TanStack Form), and the matching resolver is wired up automatically.
- **Consistent validation** — required fields, length and number bounds, email
  and URL formats, and date rules generate the same way across all three
  libraries.
