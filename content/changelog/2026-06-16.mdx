---
title: Date picker, multi-file output & field visibility
description: A new date field, cleaner generated code, and more control over your fields.
---

- **Date picker field** — add a date field with single-date or range selection,
  optional month and year dropdowns, min/max bounds, and switches to disable
  past dates or weekends. Generates code for both React Hook Form and TanStack
  Form.
- **Multi-file code output** — generated code can now span multiple files, so
  reusable pieces like a `PasswordInput` come out as their own components
  instead of being inlined.
- **Field visibility toggle** — hide a field from the form without deleting it,
  then bring it back whenever you need it.
