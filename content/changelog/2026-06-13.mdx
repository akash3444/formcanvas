---
title: Combobox field & a safer editor
description: Searchable selects, plus quality-of-life touches.
---

- **Combobox field** — a searchable dropdown for when a plain select has too many
  options.
- **Clear-form confirmation** — clearing your form now asks first, so you can't
  wipe your work by accident.
- **Tab icons** — the Preview and Code tabs are easier to tell apart at a glance.
